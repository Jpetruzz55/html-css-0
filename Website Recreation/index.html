A Review of HTML

HTML is really a very simple scripting language. It consists of ordinary text, with elements that are enclosed by "<" and ">" characters

The best part about HTML is that once we know how to effectively use basic elements, we can look up how to use all of the others!

Links

Links are very useful, and help users to quickly naviagte to new pages, both within and outside of the current website.

For instance, they can quickly direct users to Googles Homepage

Emails can also be easily sent through the use of links.

They can even bring a user to a certain place within a webpage.

Images

Sometimes we want to go ahead and align pictures to the left of a line of text. Using the "align" attribute, with a value of "left" is a perfect way to do this. You could also align the picture to the right of the text by replacing the value in the "align" attribute to "right."
